About Us

Browse through an exquisitely diverse selection of over 200 candy brands from around the world at Hello Sweets. From timeless favorites like Haribo and Ferrero to American bestsellers like Hershey's, Reese's, Nerds, and Twinkies – there's something for every palate. Thanks to our innovative Pick&Mix service, you can also create your own unique candy mixtures for a personalized snacking experience. With a product range of over 1500 items, Hello Sweets is one of Germany's leading brick-and-mortar stores for sweets, snacks, and drinks. From classic brands to nostalgic retro products, market novelties, and international delicacies, Hello Sweets offers unparalleled variety and depth. "Discover the world of the most popular and trending snacks, chocolates, drinks, and more with Hello Sweets, sourced from the fantastic candy worlds on all five continents for you." "Dive into the fascinating world of Hello Sweets' surprise boxes and discover a variety of exciting flavor combinations waiting for you in-store. Whether your taste buds prefer sour, spicy, salty, or sweet – Hello Sweets has something special for everyone." "Our goal is to be the ultimate destination for a diverse selection of candies from around the world, from American to Asian treats. We import the best sweets and drinks to Germany, placing the highest importance on quality. Each candy box is filled with top-notch products that will delight our customers. We strive to excite candy lovers across the country with the most exciting and exotic treats that have not been available in Germany until now!"
